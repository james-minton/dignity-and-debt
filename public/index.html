Why Dignity?

Why are we talking about dignity when talking about debt? Autonomy and protection from abuse. Does debt necessarily erode a person’s autonomy as she decides who she is and to behave as is she has a sense of free will? And when debt result from exploitation and abuse? Can we re-imagine a world in which debt does not have to accompany indignity?
